My Data Story: Hitting my Target with

Data Collection

The first project we were asked to complete in
Writing & Digital Media was data collection. At first,
I was a little intimidated by collecting data on a daily
basis and recording every applicable instance.
However, I soon realized that it wasn’t as difficult
as I had imagined and I actually enjoyed being able
to track my behavior with Fitbit. I tracked data in these
categories: Device, Check-Type, Activity-Type,
Decision, Rationale, Interface.

I also collected data on how many steps I walked, miles
I walked, calories I consumed, and calories I burned
along with how many and what type of emails I received
from Fitbit.

Data Visualization

After my data collection was finalized, I moved onto
designing my data visualization. This was a very fun
project for me, and also probably the most challenging.
I decided to take my data and place it in the shape of
a target, playing off of the idea that Fitbit helps me
reach my target goals. Each of the categories mentioned
above are placed around the circle and the days of the week
are represented by each of the target’s rings.

Explained Video

Finally, I produced and edited a video about psychographics,
user personas, and email marketing. The topic tied into Fitbit
because during my internship over the summer, I was exposed to
Fitbit’s email marketing campaigns and tactics. I enjoyed working
on this project because I have always liked working with video
software. It was also a chance to learn more about my field of
interest, digital marketing.
